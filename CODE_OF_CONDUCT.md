# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard
Be respectful, direct, and technically honest. Critique ideas and implementations, not people.

## Expected behavior
- Share evidence with claims.
- Document sample-data provenance clearly.
- Report limitations and uncertainty explicitly.
- Keep discussions constructive and specific.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment or personal attacks
- Publishing private packet captures or secrets
- Misrepresenting test results or sample-data origin

## Reporting
Use the repository contact path or security reporting path for serious concerns.
